My Project

I began looking for a way to create new things and a career path that would allow me unrestricted growth. I stumbled on to web developement and software engineering and was instantly hooked. A week later i sold all my belonging packed my car with my dogs and whatever i could fit and left Colorado. I drove 37 hours to get to Maine and moved into my sisters attic so that i could focus all my time and effort into learning everything I could about software engineering and this page is the sum of my knowledge up to this point I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as i enjoyed making it.
